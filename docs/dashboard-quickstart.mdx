After you've created your account on our [Dashboard](https://dashboard.uniblock.dev/) you will be greeted with our onboard process at the `Home` page and you can begin with your development journey following it:

![](/images/docs/2e2cf12e8406b18bcd26baa65e7f1bb4be8c804af2082657b05a5300aa7f373d-image.png)

### **1. Project Details**

Begin by setting up your project. Fill in your project's name, description, and include what you might be using this project for.

![](/images/docs/fe60df722b817221dde529af0dec90b1ab73609cef18db64ff84e6b1b48d24b3-image.png)

### **2. Explore Endpoints**

Explore what Uniblock endpoints has to offer for your needs.

![](/images/docs/4b80520e517b9332f8afd2834ae1f9be6bece66b83a01c68d72deb421f9ef898-image.png)

### **3. Explore Uniblock Plans**

Uniblock has different plans tailored to different levels of users. Try out with the free plan before you have it figured out and you can always upgrade anytime.

![](/images/docs/a9f80332f5ad38d59bcf9b7cd1596d4015f9c35bd1c1bf27fc561f152d24cc38-image.png)

### **4. Know the Resources**

These resources are handy to help you get familiar with Uniblock, so that you know where to look whenever questions come up.

![](/images/docs/ac377d9f059254a680f608f3eb8608d42521544083a87c2d873a74db2ce5342b-image.png)

### **5. Project Created!**

After clicking the `Finish` button, you have just created your first project on Uniblock. You will be redirect to the project overview page automatically.

<Info>
  ### Things you can do next

- Invite your team members to the project. Refer to [Team](doc:dashboard-team).
- Call an unified endpoint with your Uniblock API key to access blockchain data across networks and sourced from various providers. Refer to [Unified API Quickstart](doc:unified-api-quickstart).
- If you know about any service provider already (let's say Alchemy), you can try out calling an endpoint of that provider using Direct API. Refer to [Direct API Overview](doc:direct-api-overview).
- Create a webhook to listen to certain events occurring on blockchains. Refer to [Webhook Quickstart](doc:webhook-quickstart).
- Take a look at some of the most popular smart contract in Unified Contracts Library. You can browse the docs and code, as well as no-code deploy. Refer to [Unified Contracts Overview](doc:unified-contract-overview)

</Info>
