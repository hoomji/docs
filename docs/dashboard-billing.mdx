Users can upgrade plan anytime when run out of compute units. There are 5 tiers available for your need:

- Startup: free
- Growth: 39 USD/month
- Pro: 179 USD/month
- Business: 499 USD/month
- Custom: get in touch for a quote

### How to add payment methods

1. When on the Billing page, click on the `Payment Methods` tab.

2. Click on `Add Payment Method`.

   ![](/images/docs/a7f4f98f0a2490ec94d746f1815e5a71bb906c9360fbe3005d86a7dd5086a937-image.png)

3. Fill out the fields to add your credit card as payment method.

   ![](/images/docs/8dc7598c90f2e20f4a764dfbf8341cdf5e97d91ba2766b2e35453639d29f8d2a-image.png)

4. Now you have added a payment method.

![](/images/docs/d6b75494de84230264dbf1aae40b3bb162674113643bef6b016cb8b6eb722f35-image.png)

5. Click on the 3 dot button on the top right of the payment card, and click `Make as default`. Now your payment card is in use for the subscription fee of your project.

   ![](/images/docs/03334570abeb01d877269eddb51817704b61bf187c9f520e491006ded1a1ab63-image.png)

![](/images/docs/718e5be92167d2f8baa624ea63479c28030162a566621a00cf254027e9f13fbc-image.png)
